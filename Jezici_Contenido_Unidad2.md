## Lección 2.4 — Preguntas con *to be*
**Chunks:** Are you…?, "Yes, I am" / "No, I'm not", Is he/she…?, "What's your phone number?".

- **E1** · `cloze` · reading — "___ you a student?" → **Are**
- **E2** · `multiple_choice` · reading — Respuesta corta (sí) a "Are you from Peru?" → Yes, I do | Yes, I am | I'm fine → **Yes, I am**
- **E3** · `cloze` · writing — "No, I'm ___." → **not**
- **E4** · `multiple_choice` · reading — "*Is she your friend?* pregunta sobre…" → él | ella | tú → **ella**
- **E5** · `reorder` · writing — Ordena: you / Are / a / teacher → **Are you a teacher**
- **E6** · `translation` · writing — Traduce: "¿Eres de Brasil?" → **Are you from Brazil?**

- **E8** · `speaking_read_aloud` · speaking — Lee en voz alta: **"Are you from Peru? Yes, I am."**
